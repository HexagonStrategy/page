Kalimour is a tactical city-building game where you must raise a kingdom in hostile lands filled with wild beasts, treacherous bandits and rival lords. Expand your borders, raise an army and take charge of your new land.

Defeat your enemies and conquer the world
Protect your people from savage beasts, fend off greedy bandits and march your army against rival kingdoms as you grow your military power. Bring your force to bear on your enemies, take control of their territory and make yourself known as the true ruler of this land.

Raise an army worthy of a king
Train a wide variety of troops to fit your strategy: Elite knights that can take a beating to hold the line, Archers to keep your enemies at bay, cheap and fast Scouts to overwhelm your foes, and many more. You will face many different enemies in your campaign for conquest, so remember to change your strategy to adapt to new challenges.
